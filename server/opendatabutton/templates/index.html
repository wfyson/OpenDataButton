Open Data Button

This Law Mining Hackathon project started at OKCon 2013 aims to help lift legal and technical restrictions on data, and get rapid community response to changes of terms of use. The Open Data Button is an easy, social way to share the fact that you are prevented from accessing data in some way.

To get started, simply drag this button up to your browser's bookmark bar:

Open This Data!

Goals

  1. Develop crowdsourcing of not-so-open data
  2. Make an easy way of sharing restrictions
  3. Visualize the “not-so-open-data map”
Visit the project wiki for more information.
